About Me

My name is Genevieve, but I prefer Gen, and I'm from San Francisco, CA. I'm a senior at UC Berkeley majoring in computer science and minoring in data science, and I look forward to pursuing a career in software engineering after graduation.

My interest in all things computers started when my mom gifted me a Windows desktop computer in the second or third grade. In between soccer and karate practices, one of my favorite afternoon past times was playing computer games. Many years later, I was lucky to be admitted to the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program after my sophomore year of high school. That summer was my first introduction to computer science, and I spent seven weeks at Twitter in San Francisco learning the basics of Scratch, Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and jQuery. I was completely hooked, and I guess you could say the rest is history!

In my free time, I enjoy dancing and baking, and I'm always down for Vietnamese food and boba! Below are some photos of my performances with my dance teams, AFX Boom and AFX Mo!
