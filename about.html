About Me

My name is Genevieve, but I prefer Gen, and I'm from San Francisco, CA. I graduated from UC Berkeley in Fall 2020 with a BA in Computer Science. I am currently seeking a career in software engineering in the SF Bay Area. My skill set is mainly backend, but my goal is to do frontend as I have gotten into web design!

My interest in all things computers started when my mom gifted me a Windows desktop computer in the second or third grade. In between soccer and karate practices, one of my favorite afternoon past times was playing computer games like Wizard101 and the PC version of Lego Star Wars. Many years later, I was lucky to be admitted to the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program after my sophomore year of high school. That summer was my first introduction to computer science, and I spent seven weeks at Twitter in San Francisco learning the basics of Scratch, Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and jQuery. I was completely hooked, and I guess you could say the rest is history!

Some fun facts about me are...

  • I have a twin sister, but we're fraternal so it's kind of hard to tell.
  • I like to cook! My best dishes are kimchi fried rice and Japanese curry.
  • I also like to bake! My favorites are banana bread (I haven't found anyone who dislikes my mom's family recipe) and brownie cookies.
  • While I like both pho and ramen, I'm definitely team pho over team ramen. My favorite place to get pho in San Francisco is Kevin's Noodle House (Pho Huynh Hiep 2).
  • My favorite boba place is Boba Guys, and the strawberry tea fresca with boba is my favorite thing on their menu!
